Privacy Policy

How AGAT Partners LLC, operating as Dorvelon, collects, uses, and safeguards information across the dorvelon.com website and engagement lifecycle.

Effective May 5, 2026

01 — Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how AGAT Partners LLC, operating as Dorvelon (“Dorvelon,” “we,” “us”), collects, uses, and safeguards information when you visit dorvelon.com, schedule a consultation, or otherwise engage with the practice. It does not apply to third-party platforms we link to, which operate under their own policies.

02 — Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to evaluate fit, schedule engagements, and meet professional and legal obligations. This includes: (a) identifying information you provide voluntarily — name, professional email, organization, and role; (b) scheduling information processed through our consultation tool (Cal.com); (c) limited technical information from your browser, such as IP address, user agent, and referring URL, used for security and aggregate analytics. We do not collect financial account information through this site.

03 — How We Use Information

Information is used to respond to inquiries, scope and deliver engagements, maintain records required by professional standards, and comply with applicable law. We do not sell personal information and do not use it for behavioral advertising.

04 — Engagement Confidentiality

Information shared during a paid engagement is governed by the executed engagement letter and any non-disclosure agreement between the parties. Those terms supersede this policy with respect to engagement-specific materials.

05 — Cookies and Analytics

The site uses strictly necessary cookies for core functionality and may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate usage. Where consent is required by your jurisdiction, we will request it before non-essential cookies are set.

06 — Service Providers

We rely on a small set of vetted vendors — including hosting (Vercel), scheduling (Cal.com), and email — bound by their own data protection commitments. We disclose information to them only to the extent necessary to operate the site or deliver engagements.

07 — Data Retention

Inquiry and engagement records are retained for the duration of the relationship and for the period required to satisfy legal, regulatory, audit, and record-keeping obligations. Records are then securely deleted or anonymized.

08 — Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information, and to object to or withdraw consent for certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below. We will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law.

09 — International Transfers

Dorvelon is based in the United States. If you contact us from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States under appropriate safeguards.

10 — Security

We maintain administrative, technical, and physical safeguards commensurate with the sensitivity of the information we hold. No system is perfectly secure, and we encourage clients to use the secure channels established at engagement onset for sensitive materials.

11 — Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. Material changes will be reflected by an updated effective date at the top of this page.

12 — Contact

Questions about this policy or our handling of personal information may be directed to AGAT Partners LLC via the consultation channel listed on this site. For engagement-specific privacy inquiries, please reference the relevant engagement letter.

This Privacy Policy is provided for informational purposes and is not legal advice. Engagement-specific terms govern the handling of client materials.
